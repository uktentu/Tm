03 — Training Protocol

The Toji Build

Lean, broad, strong forearms. 5-day hypertrophy split. Paired with mind-muscle cues on every set.

Push Day
Monday
Chest · Shoulders · Triceps
7
Exercises

Mind-Muscle Coordination

  • Before every set: 5-second mental rehearsal, feel the target muscle
  • 3-second controlled eccentric on every rep — this is where growth happens
  • 1-second peak contraction at the top — do not skip this
  • Between sets: place your hand on the worked muscle and feel the pump
  • If you cannot feel it working, reduce weight and slow down

Progressive Overload Rules

  • Add weight only when you hit the top of the rep range with perfect form
  • Increase by the smallest increment available — 2.5 kg per side is fine
  • If reps drop, stay at the same weight until you hit them again
  • Track your lifts — if it is not written down, it did not happen
  • Volume before intensity: master the movement before loading it heavy

05 — Personal Style

Dress With Intent

Fashion is just consistent application of rules. Lean frame, athletic potential, dark palette.

01
The Foundation (Buy First)
  • Three plain crew-neck tees: black, white, charcoal — slim fit, not skin-tight
  • Two henley shirts in olive and navy for layering
  • One black slim-fit jean, one dark indigo; one black chino, one beige
  • One black overshirt or chore jacket — the most versatile layer you own
  • Clean white low-top sneakers and black low-tops
02
Fit Rules for Lean Frames
  • Shirt sleeves end mid-bicep, never at the elbow
  • Shoulder seam sits exactly on your shoulder bone — non-negotiable
  • T-shirt length ends at mid-zipper of your jeans
  • Jeans break once at the ankle, nothing more
  • Avoid baggy — you look smaller, not bigger
  • Layering adds visual mass: tee plus open shirt beats tee alone
03
Your Color Palette
  • Three neutrals to build around: black, white, beige or khaki
  • Two accent colors: deep navy and olive green
  • One outfit equals three colors maximum — more reads chaotic
  • Dark colors slim. Lighter colors add visual mass for legs
  • No logos, graphics, or slogans until you have built taste
04
Hair and Grooming
  • Haircut every 4 to 5 weeks, short on sides, length on top
  • Trim or grow facial hair deliberately — patchy stubble reads as lazy
  • Eyebrows: comb daily, trim strays — the most underrated change
  • Nails clipped and clean, always, without exception
05
Skincare (5 min/day)
  • Morning: cleanser → moisturizer → SPF 50 (even indoors)
  • Night: cleanser → niacinamide → moisturizer
  • Twice weekly: chemical exfoliant — BHA for oily, AHA for dry skin
  • Indian skin brands: Minimalist, The Ordinary, Cetaphil, Plum
06
Daily Decision Framework
  • Pick top first, then a neutral bottom — always in that order
  • Layer when in doubt — adds depth and intention
  • Always one anchor item: watch, chain, or quality jacket
  • Cannot decide in 90 seconds? Black tee, black jeans, white sneakers
